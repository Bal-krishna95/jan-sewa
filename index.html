About Guruji Jan Sewa Kendra

We are a trusted government service centre and book store serving the people of Lucknow for over 10 years. Our mission is to make government paperwork simple, affordable, and accessible for everyone.

"हम हर परिवार की सरकारी ज़रूरतें पूरी करने के लिए यहाँ हैं।"

  • Government-authorised Jan Sewa Kendra operator
  • 15+ document and certificate services under one roof
  • Large collection of school, exam & religious books
  • Hindi & English speaking staff — no confusion
  • Affordable fees — no hidden charges
  • Same-day processing for most certificates
